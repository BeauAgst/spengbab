The "Mocking Spongebob" Slackbot

Spengbab is a Slackbot that implements the "Mocking Spongebob" meme in your channels.

FAQ

  • How do I use the app?

    Simple enter the command /spengbab, followed by the phrase that you want converted.

  • How does this app use my data?

    It doesn't. It collects nothing on you, and just converts your text.

  • What access does the app need?

    None. It literally implements the single /spengbab slash command.

  • Does this app ever mention @everyone?

    Nope! It simply returns back whatever the user enters.
